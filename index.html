about!

I'm currently an undergraduate student studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Calgary and my anticipated graduation is in April 2028.

I enjoy analyzing complex systems and getting to work on problems that make you scratch your head and require some out-of-the-box thinking. I also really like math so anything with lots of that appeals to me! In my free time, I'm an avid reader (fantasy and thriller fans rise), cyclist, and hobby-collector and am always looking for ways to integrate my interests with my passion for engineering.

Throughout my journey, I've experimented with various fields of mechanical engineering through research, extracurricular work, and personal projects including mechatronics, CFD, and even plain old coding (like you see on here!). While I still love learning new things and enjoy being a Jack of all trades, currently my career aspirations lie in integrating mechanical systems with embedded ones and getting to work on all parts of the process, including circuitry, coding, and product optimization.
